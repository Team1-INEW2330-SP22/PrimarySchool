Creating/Enrolling a New Student

To create a new student and enroll them in the school, navigate to the Students Form from the Main Menu by going to Academic Officer -> Students.

 

By default, the Students form does not display any records when first opened.

1. To add a new student to the application, click the “Add New” button. This will enable the records fields and allow the entry of a new student record.

2. Complete the form with the information of the student to add. Below are some requirements for each field:

• All fields are required

• Date of Birth: Must be in the date Format YYYY-MM-DD

• Zip: Must be 5 characters in length

3. Once all fields are entered, click the “Save” button to complete the entry and save the student’s record. The fields are disabled to prevent data from being edited. To edit the student’s data, follow the steps in the Editing an Existing student portion of this document.

The Student ID field is an automatically generated number by the application and cannot be changed or selected. This is a unique number that is used throughout the application..

To apply student Credentials, follow the steps outlined in the Applying student Roles portion of this document..
