Update a Course's Gradebook

Gradebook Design

The gradebook form is seperated into three sections including the course selection and details, student assignment list, and the student final grades. The grades in the Final Grades frame is a calculated value based on each student's average grade among all of their graded assignments.

A course can been selected by using the Select Course dropdown field and selected the course. Selecting a course will display the course details including the course name, assigned teacher, and the assign room. It will also populate the assignment list per each student and the list of students with their final grades. The list of students is ordered in descending order from highest grade to lowest.

Grades and comments can be applied to each student's assignment by selecting the grade and comments fields next to the respective student's assignment.
